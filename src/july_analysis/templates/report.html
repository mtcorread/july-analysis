Channel efficiency — events vs infections

For every encounter type: how many real events fired (n_events, after collapsing pair-records back to one row per event via group_id), how many non-seed infections were attributed to that channel, and their ratio. A small infections_per_event means either low per-event transmission probability (contact-matrix / fomite story) OR that most events had no susceptible-infectious mix (eligibility / epidemic-timing story). A large ratio combined with a small n_events means the channel is sparse but efficient. Compare group_sex's ratio against the other sexual channels to sanity-check that sparse group-sex transmissions are consistent with the other sexual channels' per-event rates.

Encounter mix
